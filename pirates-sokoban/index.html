About the Game

Pirates Sokoban is a simple pirate-themed puzzle game — my very first attempt using Godot! This is also my first submission to the Solana dApp Store.
There are no ads, no blockchain features, and it’s completely free to play.
I made this purely for the challenge and to see it come to life… enjoy the adventure! :)
